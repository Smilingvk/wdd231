Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Arequipa Artisan Bakery

This name was chosen because it's direct and informative. It immediately communicates the business type (Artisan Bakery) and its geographical location (Arequipa), making it highly relevant for local customers and search engine optimization.

Optional domain availability: arequipaartisan.pe

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as the digital storefront for the Arequipa Artisan Bakery. It will attract new customers and serve existing ones by:

  • Showcasing our daily and specialty baked goods with enticing descriptions and photos.
  • Providing essential information such as our opening hours, address, and contact details.
  • Allowing customers to place online orders for custom cakes and bulk items for pickup.

Scenarios

These are questions that a typical visitor to the site might have:

  • "Do you offer any gluten-free products?"
  • "How can I order a custom birthday cake for next week?"
  • "What are your hours on public holidays?"
